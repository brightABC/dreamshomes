ABOUT US

OUR STORY

At Dream House, our journey began with a simple yet profound observation: the struggle to find affordable and comfortable accommodation in and around the Federal University of Lafia. As students and staff ourselves, we understood the challenges of navigating a limited housing market, exacerbated by the scarcity of hostel space.

We saw friends and classmates struggling to secure accommodation, often having to settle for suboptimal options or commute long distances. We realized that this wasn't just a logistical issue, but also a significant stressor that affected academic performance and overall well-being.

This sparked an idea: to create a platform that would connect property owners, agents, and potential tenants, making it easier to find and book accommodation that meets individual needs. We set out to build a user-friendly, comprehensive, and reliable real estate website hat would cater specifically to the Federal University of Lafia community.

DreamsHomes was born out of a passion to simplify accomodation search process for students. Our founders, who are students themselves, understood the challenges of finding a reliable and affordable place to stay while pursuing higher education. With a vision to make student accomodation eaiser, safer, and create a more affordable, we set out to create a platform that connects students with verified and trustworthy landlords. Today, Dreams Homes is a leading online platform for student accomodation, dedicated to helping students find their dream home.

OUR MISSION

At Dream House, our mission is to provide innovative and practical solutions to the housing needs of students, staff, and faculty at the Federal University of Lafia. We want to provide a seamless and stress-free accomodation research experience for students. We believe that every student deserve a comfortable and safe place to call home.

OUR SERVICES

-VERIFIED LISTINGS: We partner with trustworthy landlords to offer verified and accurate listings of student accomodations.

-USER-FRIENDLY PLATFORM: Our website is designed to be easy to use allowing to search, compare and book accomodations with use.

-SECURE PAYMENTS: We offer secure payment options, ensuring that students can book their accomodations with confidence.

-ONGOING SUPPORT: Our team is dedicated to providing ongoing support and resources to students, helping them with any issues or concerns they may have during their stay.

-LANDLORD SERVICES: We also offer services to landlords, including property listing, tenant screening and property management.
