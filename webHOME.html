How it works...

Find your perfect landed property



Browse Accomodations

Students search for apartments on the website using search filters such as location. They can view photos, descriptions and reviews of properties to find best option.

Booking a website Tour

Once students find a choice, they can view the full listing, including details on amenities, pricing, and landlord information and book a viewing through the website.

Documetation

After booking a property, students can make secure payments through the website's payment gateway, and then receive documentation outlining T's & C's of agreement.

Move in and Enjoy

Once payment/doc. are complete, students can move into their new home! We will also offer customer support to help students with issues during stay.
